About Us

Tiny Techies is an educational initiative dedicated to empowering kindergarten-level students with the skills and mindset to thrive in a technology-driven world. We introduce young learners to programming fundamentals and guide them in creating simple projects using Internet of Things (IoT) devices, fostering curiosity, creativity, and problem-solving abilities through engaging and age-appropriate activities.

Our curriculum is designed specifically for young children, using visual programming languages, interactive games, and hands-on projects that make learning technology concepts fun and accessible.

Our Vision

Our vision is to inspire the next generation of innovators by making technology education accessible and exciting for children from their earliest years. We believe that every child should have the opportunity to develop digital literacy and computational thinking skills that will serve them throughout their lives.
